About Waterford

Waterford is a city in the southeast of Ireland, it is renowed for its rich history, culture and natural landscapes.As Waterford is the oldest city in Ireland, it has a fascinating heritage dating back over a thousand years.With structures such as Reginald's Tower and the iconic Waterford Crystal factory.Visitors to Waterford can explore its historic sites,enjoy scenic walks along the River Suir, and indulge in the citys culture.Waterford offers a memorable experience for travelers that are looking for a wonderful journey.
